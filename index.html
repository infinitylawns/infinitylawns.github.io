~ Our Services ~

Mowing

Good Lawn care starts with regular mowing. We take pride in providing you with the best lawn care around. Not only will we cut your grass, we also do the edges and blow your drives and paths clear.

Fertilising

A Good Fertiliser applied at the right time can make vastly improve your lawns look and health. Healthy lawns not only look great and feel great under foot, but can also add alot of value to your property. Our fertilisers contain a range of organic elements, biological stimulants and specialist chemicals sourced from commercial greenkeeping suppliers.

Weed Control

Weeds such as flatgrass , clover and bindii's are regular visitors to even the best kept lawns. Not only do they look unsightly but given time can quickly spread and out compete your lawn for nutrients, water and sun. Our sprays are targeted to remove the unwanted weeds and leave your lawn weed free.

Pest Control

Lawn diseases and pests such as army worm, cut worm, and white curl grub can quickly become a big problem for any lawn, killing off acres of lawn in as little as a week. The right spray done at the right time can help manage and save your lawn from the threat of these pests and their devastating effects.

Turfing

Wish you had a great new lawn or perhaps need to cover that ugly bare dirt patch. We do all aspects of turfing from removal of the old lawn, the preparation and even spread of a quality underlay, and the delivery and installation of your new lawn. If your not sure which lawn to get, we can provide you with best solutions.

Top Dressing

Is your lawn looking tired and worn out. Topdressing greatly reduces lawn stresses, helps to keep thatch under control, and acts as a long-term natural fertilizer.
Ask Jack about his lawn top dressing, it's a great way to bring your lawn to life.

Irrigation

Do you need to make sure your lawn or garden gets adequate moisture. Don't let the summer heat take it's toll, we can install or repair any irrigation to your lawn and plants.

Drainage

Tired of having that boggy patch of lawn that never dries, or unwanted pools of water when it rains.
We can assess and implement drainage solutions to your gardens and lawns to help deal with your unwanted water.
